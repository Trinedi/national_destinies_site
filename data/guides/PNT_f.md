## Concept

The Pintados Confederation is the pan-Visayan tattoo-warrior civilization,
the political project that the historical Madya-as confederation of
Panay datus and the Rajahnate of Cebu hinted at but never unified.
Pintados (the Painted Ones) is the Spanish term for the heavily tattooed
Visayan warriors the conquistadors first encountered, the kakarlong
brotherhoods who waged mangayaw raids from balangay and karakoa fleets
across the inner seas.

The mod's framing is a federation of datus bound by sandugo (the
blood-compact), a maritime confederation of three Visayan languages
(Hiligaynon, Cebuano, Waray), and a babaylan-led anitist religious
order. It is explicitly not a Madya-as enlargement: it is the unified
Visayan polity that history almost reached.

The tag is PNT (after Pintados). It excludes the Mindanao Moros (handled
by MRO), the Tagalogs of Manila Bay, and the Bicolanos.

## Forming it

1. **Start as a Visayan-cultured tag.** Eligible primary cultures are
   Hiligaynon (MDY), Cebuano (CEB), and Waray. No tag at game start
   has Waray as primary, so practical starters are MDY and CEB.
2. **Hold both required locations.** The `allow` block requires you
   to own **Araut** (held by MDY) and **Singhapala** (held by CEB).
   Whichever you start as, conquer the other.
3. **Hold two of the four secondary anchors.** You must own at least
   two of **Basey** (Samar), **Bool** (Bohol, held by BOL), **Bago
   Negros** (Negros, held by MDY), and **Masbate**. This is a
   deliberate breadth requirement to enforce pan-Visayan unification
   rather than a Panay plus Cebu duopoly.
4. **Hit 85 percent of the Visayas area.** The fraction is high; you
   need almost the whole Visayas pool.
5. **Not in civil war.**

The formable does not gate religion. CEB starts hindu and qualifies
on culture alone, but the mod's flavor and advance tree assume an
anitist polity (the babaylan, the diwata, anito worship under the
Babaylan order).

## Pick your founding doctrine on formation

The formation event offers three 20-year doctrines:

- **Federation of the Datus.** The council civilization: governance,
  cohesion, council legitimacy, datus bound by sandugo. Pulls society
  toward plutocracy. Unlocks the Civic Council destiny path (United
  Council, Visayan Renaissance, Kabisay-an Apex).
- **Kakarlong Brotherhood.** The mangayaw raid tradition, the tattoo
  warrior code, balangay raid fleets. Pulls society toward belligerence.
  Unlocks the Maritime Raid Empire destiny path (Sulu Sea Mastery,
  Borneo Beachheads, Spice Sea Talassocracy).
- **Maritime Confederation.** The balangay merchant network, inner-sea
  free-passage trade. Pulls society toward naval. **No destiny
  continuation.** Pick this only if you want the founding modifier
  without committing to either destiny path.

## What happens on formation

- Mild stability bonus.
- Formation event fires with the three doctrine choices.
- No automatic capital change, culture flip, or rank promotion.

## After forming

The destiny tree splits two ways:

- **Datu Council Civilization** (Path A). Bind the datus by sworn law
  or by sandugo, sponsor the Visayan Renaissance with patronage of the
  arts and the three Visayan tongues (Hiligaynon, Cebuano, Waray) in
  parallel, and crown the realm as the Kabisay-an Apex, a civic nation
  or a mercantile federation. Unlocked by the Federation of the Datus
  formation choice.
- **Maritime Raid Empire** (Path B). Master the Sulu Sea via Iranun
  corsair commissions or council admiralty integration, land mangayaw
  raid corps in Borneo, and crown the realm as a Spice Sea
  Talassocracy with reaver lords or inner-sea dominion. Unlocked by
  the Kakarlong Brotherhood formation choice.

## Notes

- The Maritime Confederation founding choice is a deliberate dead-end.
  Pick it only for the founding modifier itself; neither destiny path
  continues from it.
- Several advances fire DHE flavor events: the Code of Kalantiaw /
  Sumakwel maragtas codex, the Mangayaw Decision (raid south or north),
  the Babaylan Question (codify or pluralism), the Visayan Sea Trade
  Treaty, the Spaniards at Mactan (post-1521), and the Visayan Synthesis.
- The Visayan Synthesis advance lets you choose between three flourishing
  Visayan tongues and a single shared council vernacular, a real
  language-policy fork inside the destiny payoff.
