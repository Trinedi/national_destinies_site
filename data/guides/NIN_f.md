## Concept

The Assyrian Empire revives the Neo-Assyrian imperial peak under
Esarhaddon and Ashurbanipal, channelled through medieval Syriac
Christian institutions. The historical reference is the 671 BC Sargonid
peak: direct rule over northern Mesopotamia, Babylonia, Elam, the
Levant, Cyprus, and Egypt itself (Memphis taken 671 BC, Thebes sacked
663 BC), with tributary reach across Anatolia and western Iran.

The medieval framing makes the Patriarchate of the East the political
nucleus of the imperial revival. The Catholicos at Seleucia crowns the
Assyrian emperor at the rebuilt Akitu altar. Aramaic civil service,
Bar Hebraeus library scholarship, and Tur Abdin monastic defense from
the BNH stage become the foundation institutions of a Sargonid-tier
empire.

The tag is NIN (after Nineveh) because vanilla EU5 already uses ASR
for Astarabad in Persia.

## Forming it

There are two paths.

### Path 1: Form BNH first, then NIN

1. **Form Beth Nahrain (BNH)** following the BNH guide. Pick a small
   Caucasian Armenian principality or ATZ, conquer Mosul, Nusaybin,
   and Urfa, and form the Tier 1 duchy.
2. **At BNH formation, choose Tur Abdin Defenders or Patriarchal
   Restoration.** Both unlock destiny paths. Tur Abdin Defenders flows
   into the Restoration of Aššur destiny path which signposts toward
   NIN.
3. **Build out BNH's heritage tree.** Standing army prerequisites,
   riverine logistics, monastic recruitment, qanat infrastructure.
4. **Conquer the imperial peak.** The five required locations are
   Mosul, Baghdad, Damascus, Cairo, and either Shush (ancient Susa,
   the eastern frontier per Ashurbanipal) or Tarsus (the Cilician
   frontier per Sargon II). Plus 60 percent of the Crescent and Egypt
   regions and the Khuzestan and Cilicia areas.
5. **Form NIN.**

### Path 2: Cilicia direct

1. **Start as Cilicia.** Already miaphysite, already Kingdom rank,
   already adjacent to the Edessa frontier.
2. **Skip BNH entirely.** Cilicia at Kingdom rank is too high for the
   Tier 1 gate. Go straight for the L4 conquest.
3. **Take all five required locations** (Mosul, Baghdad, Damascus,
   Cairo, plus Shush or Tarsus) and the 60 percent fraction.
4. **Form NIN.** Country culture flips from Armenian to Syriac on
   formation.

The chain path gives you twenty-five years of BNH's Tier 1 institutions
plus a built-out heritage tree before the imperial conquest begins.
The Cilicia direct path is faster and avoids the detour but starts the
imperial campaign without the BNH investments.

## Pick your founding doctrine on formation

The formation event offers three 40-year doctrines:

- **Sargonid Imperium.** The Neo-Assyrian war machine: kisir sharruti
  standing army, qurubuti royal guard, siege engineering. Stat focus:
  morale, discipline, siege ability, manpower, attrition reduction.
- **Pax Assyriaca.** The Sargonid administrative system: provincial
  governance, Aramaic civil service, deportation and integration as
  imperial practice. Stat focus: cabinet efficiency, control,
  assimilation speed, integration speed, infrastructure.
- **The Cross and the Lamassu.** Civilizational synthesis. Patriarch
  and emperor in one ritual, ancient Assyrian symbolism reframed
  Christian. Stat focus: tolerance, cultural influence, prestige,
  missionary networks.

## What happens on formation

- Capital moves to Mosul (Nineveh).
- Country culture flips to Syriac for non-Syriac formers. State pops
  of every plausible Eastern conquered culture (Armenian, Iraqi,
  Kurdish, Levantine, Turkish, Farsi, Coptic, both Egyptian variants)
  convert to Syriac.
- Country rank rises to Empire if below it.
- Severe stability bonus.
- Formation event fires with the three doctrine choices.

## After forming

The destiny tree splits two ways:

- **Pax Sargonica.** Push beyond the historical Neo-Assyrian peak.
  Required conquests reach Anatolia (Konya, Kayseri, Ankara, Smyrna),
  deeper Iran (Hamadan, Istakhr), and the Achaemenid east (Samarkand,
  Peshawar). Capstone building is Bīt-Mātāti, the Hall of the Lands,
  a universal-empire throne hall. Unlocked by the Sargonid Imperium
  formation choice.
- **New Mesopotamia.** Civilizational hegemon. Empire-wide Beth Hekma
  libraries, Aramaic as a global lingua franca, Christian missionary
  networks from Iberia to China, Tigris-Euphrates riverine
  infrastructure as the world's economic spine. Capstone building is
  the Imperial Canal of Sennacherib. Unlocked by The Cross and the
  Lamassu formation choice.

The Pax Assyriaca formation choice has no destiny continuation. Pick
it only if you want the formation modifier without committing to
either destiny path.
