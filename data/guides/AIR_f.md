## Concept

The Sultanate of Air revives the historical Tuareg confederation
centred on the Air Mountains and the caravan city of Agadez. The
historical reference is the Sultanate of Agadez established in the
fifteenth century, which knit together the Kel Ayr clans into a
trading and raiding power straddling the trans-Saharan routes between
the Maghreb, Hausaland, and the Niger bend.

The mod frames Air as the political expression of Tuareg desert
sovereignty: the Ihaggaren noble caste in tension with the cosmopolitan
merchant city of Agadez, and the Tuareg strategic question of whether
to dominate the Sahara by the sword or by the salt and gold caravan.

## Forming it

1. **Start as a Tuareg-cultured tag** (TRG, TMK, or TMB). All three are
   tagged in the formation event as intended formers.
2. **Hold the Air area at 75 percent.** The formable's only required
   geography is `air_area`, which is just one area in the Sahel:
   the Air, Ader, and Marandet provinces. TRG already starts with
   most of it.
3. **Form Air.** No religion or rank gate, just the territory and
   Tuareg culture.

## Pick your founding doctrine on formation

The formation event offers two 15-year doctrines:

- **Lords of the desert.** The Ihaggaren warrior path. Tuareg dominion
  by the sword, raiding and tribute as the basis of the confederation.
  Unlocks Path A (Hausaland Campaign, Fall of Kano, Saharan Empire).
- **Gateway of the Sahara.** Agadez as the cosmopolitan trade hub.
  Caravan wealth, caravan protection, and control of the trans-Saharan
  routes. Unlocks Path B (Salt Road, Fezzan Link, Master of the Routes).

## What happens on formation

- A 15-year formation modifier from your chosen path.
- Mild stability bonus.
- The destiny tree unlocks in the Traditions tab, branching into
  the war or trade path you committed to.

## After forming

The destiny tree splits two ways:

- **Path A (Saharan Empire).** Hausaland campaign, Fall of Kano,
  Saharan Empire capstone. Eternal Raiders or Desert Emperor as the
  twenty-year endgame modifier. Pick this if you took Lords of the
  Desert and want to convert Tuareg cavalry into a Sahelian military
  empire reaching south into Hausa country.
- **Path B (Master of the Routes).** Salt Road, Fezzan Link, Master
  of the Routes capstone. Gold and Salt or Cosmopolitan Hub as the
  endgame modifier. Pick this if you took Gateway of the Sahara and
  want Agadez as a Mediterranean-to-Sahel trade pivot.
