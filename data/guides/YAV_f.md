## Concept

The Yavana Empire is a revival of the Indo-Greek Buddhist legacy of
Bactria and Gandhara, refracted through a Sindhi political base.
Forming it flips your primary culture from Sindhi to Yavana and
locks in either a martial *Phalanx Reborn* path or a contemplative
*Dharma of the Yavana* path through the formation event.

The formation gates are unusual: your country must be **Sindhi
culture** and follow the **Sammitiya** Buddhist sect. No tag starts
as Sammitiya, so the religion flip is part of the run, not a
prerequisite you can shop for at game start.

## Forming it

1. **Start as a Sindhi tag** (SND, SMA, or KHZ).
2. **Convert to Sammitiya.** Sindhi-culture tags begin Sunni or Shia,
   so you need a religion flip into the Buddhist family and onto the
   Sammitiya sect specifically. Easiest path: religious revolt,
   missionary conversion of your capital, or an estate-driven
   conversion event chain.
3. **Hold the cores.** The `allow` block requires you to own
   **Thatta** (Sindh) and **Peshawar** (Punjab). SND already starts
   with Thatta; Peshawar belongs to a Punjabi neighbour and must be
   taken by war or vassalisation.
4. **Hit 65% of Sindh + Punjab.** Beyond the two named locations,
   you need a fraction of the wider territory pool.
5. **Pick your path on formation.** The formation event offers
   *Phalanx Reborn* (military, quality-leaning society) or
   *Dharma of the Yavana* (spiritual, humanist-leaning).

## Notes

- The formation event auto-converts every Sindhi pop you own to
  Yavana culture, so don't grind cultural conversion before forming.
- Capital is force-set to Peshawar on formation.
