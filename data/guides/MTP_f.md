## Concept

Mutapa is the Shona empire that succeeded Great Zimbabwe: a plateau
kingdom built on cattle, gold, and the Mwari cult, its kings ruling
from stone enclosures and trading gold down the Zambezi to Swahili and
later Portuguese ports. Forming it sets your capital to Great Zimbabwe
itself and pushes you to kingdom rank.

The two paths split between **Golden Trade** (engagement with Swahili
and Portuguese coastal commerce) and **Mwari Sovereignty** (inward,
spiritual and pastoral autarky).

## Forming it

1. **Start as a Shona or Kalanga tag** (ZMW, MNY, ZEZ, NBY, or KLG).
   ZMW is the only one that already owns Great Zimbabwe; the rest must
   take it.
2. **Hold Great Zimbabwe.** This is the single allow-block requirement.
3. **Hit 60% of the Zimbabwe region.** A whole region rather than a few
   areas, so expect to consume most of your Shona and Kalanga
   neighbours and push toward the Limpopo, Save, and Zambezi.
4. **Pick your path on formation.**
   - *Let the gold flow*: Swahili and Portuguese trade, coastal
     commercial emphasis.
   - *Mwari provides*: inward spiritual focus, pastoral and ritual
     authority.

## Pick your founding doctrine on formation

The formation event offers two 30-year doctrines. Picking one also gates
which destiny path you can later take.

- **Let the gold flow** (*Golden Trade*, 30 years). Engagement with
  Swahili gold-cloth-glass commerce and managed Portuguese contact at
  Sofala as the institutional core. Stat focus: trade efficiency,
  merchant power, gold output, diplomatic reputation, research speed,
  institution growth. Gates the Emporium of the South destiny path
  (path A, Indian Ocean line).
- **Mwari provides** (*Mwari Sovereignty*, 30 years). The Matobo Hills
  rain cult, mass-mobilization armies, pastoral wealth, and oracular
  intelligence as the institutional core. Stat focus: land morale,
  manpower, legitimacy, tolerance, food production, crown estate
  power. Gates the Stones of the Ancestors destiny path (path B,
  Plateau Imperium line).

## What happens on formation

- Country rank rises to Kingdom if currently Duchy or below (ZMW
  already starts at rank_kingdom).
- Capital is force-set to Great Zimbabwe.
- Mild stability bonus.
- Formation event fires with the two doctrine choices.

## After forming

The destiny tree splits two ways:

- **Path A: Emporium of the South.** Trade line. Stage 1 takes Kilwa
  Kisiwani, the historical gold gateway. Stage 2 takes Zanzibar Island
  and Quelimane, locking in the Mozambique Channel. Stage 3 reaches
  Mombasa with capital-market porcelain access and unlocks the **Indian
  Ocean Emporium** (`nd_mtp_indian_ocean_emporium`) capstone building.
  Unlocked by the Let the gold flow formation choice.
- **Path B: Stones of the Ancestors.** Plateau conquest line. Stage 1
  marches south into Mapulana and Bakgatla. Stage 2 takes Kweneng and
  Lejweleputswa, conquering the Highveld at 2000 development. Stage 3
  takes Damara to reach the Atlantic-side frontier and unlocks the
  **Royal Mustering Ground** (`nd_mtp_royal_mustering_ground`) capstone
  building. Unlocked by the Mwari provides formation choice.

## Notes

- Capital is force-set to Great Zimbabwe on formation.
- Rank is set to kingdom if you are below it; ZMW already starts at
  rank_kingdom.
- The 30-year formation modifier rewards forming early.
- Later advance events cover the Rozvi breakaway under Changamire Dombo
  and other Mutapa milestones.
