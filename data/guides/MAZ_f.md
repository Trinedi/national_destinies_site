## Concept

Mazovia is the easternmost Piast duchy, the corner of greater Poland
that stayed independent longest. The historical Duchy of Masovia
remained outside the Crown until 1526, when its last Piast dukes died
without heirs and Sigismund I incorporated Warsaw and the Mazovian
voivodeships into the Kingdom of Poland. The Mazovian formable in
EU5 is the alternate-history outcome: the Piast cadet line survives,
Warsaw becomes a capital instead of a regional court, and the Vistula
trade is Mazovian property rather than Polish.

The formable is gated to mazovian culture only, so this is not a
greater-Polish project. It is the small-state run, with all the
flavour of an independent Vistula duchy holding its ground between
Poland, Lithuania, and the Teutonic Order.

## Forming it

You must be mazovian culture and own Warsaw. The 90 percent fraction
on the Mazovia area is what makes this a campaign rather than a button
press: at start, the Mazovia area is split between CZK (Warsaw and
the south), PLK (Plock and the north), and RAW (Rawa and the east).

1. **Start as a Mazovian duchy.** CZK, PLK, or RAW.
2. **Unify the duchy.** Take the other two duchies' Mazovian
   locations. CZK already has Warsaw; PLK and RAW need to take it.
3. **Form MAZ.** The Vistula corridor is yours.

The trick is staying alive in the corridor between POL, LIT, and the
Teutonic Order long enough to do the unification. Vassalisation under
POL is the obvious shield, but the formable does not require
independence, so a Polish vassal Mazovia can still form when ready.

## Pick your founding doctrine on formation

The formation event offers two 15-year doctrines:

- **Legacy of the Last Piasts.** Dynastic flavour, Piast bloodline as
  political legitimacy, dense petty nobility rallied to ducal
  banners. Unlocks Path A (Piast Border March -> Mazovian Crusade ->
  Shield of the Piasts), the martial path that culminates in either
  Piast Hegemony or a Frontier Bulwark capstone.
- **Gate of the Vistula.** Commercial flavour, Warsaw as a river
  emporium, grain and amber profits. Unlocks Path B (Baltic Outlet ->
  Vistula Commerce -> Crown of the Vistula), the trade path that
  culminates in either Warsaw Emporium or a Vistula league.

## What happens on formation

- Country tag becomes MAZ.
- 15-year founding modifier from your event choice.
- The duchy holds the Mazovia area in full and a 90 percent control
  share has just been verified by the trigger.

## After forming

Two destiny paths, gated by your formation choice:

- **Path A: Shield of the Piasts.** Piast Border March -> Mazovian
  Crusade (Teutonic Spoils or Szlachta Banners modifiers) -> Shield
  of the Piasts (Piast Hegemony or Frontier Bulwark). Anti-Teutonic,
  anti-steppe, dense-cavalry nobility politics.
- **Path B: Crown of the Vistula.** Baltic Outlet -> Vistula Commerce
  (Grain Fortune or Amber Road modifiers) -> Crown of the Vistula
  (Warsaw Emporium or Vistula League). Mazovia as the Hanseatic-style
  hub of the Vistula trade, leveraging grain exports through Gdansk.

## Notes

- Mazovia is Level 1 with 90 percent fraction; that fraction is the
  hard part, not the level. Plan the diplomatic order to take Warsaw
  and the smaller Mazovian duchies before any general expansion.
