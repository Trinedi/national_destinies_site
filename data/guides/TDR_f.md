## Concept

The Bosporan Kingdom is a Crimean Greco-Gothic successor state
built on top of the Principality of Theodoro, the cliff-fortress
duchy at Mangup that survived from the late Roman frontier into the
1470s. The historical references are layered: the ancient Bosporan
Kingdom of the Spartocids and Mithridates VI on the Cimmerian
Strait, the Crimean Goths (the late echo of Wulfila's people, still
speaking a Germanic tongue when Ogier de Busbecq met them at the
Ottoman court in the 1560s), and the Pontic Orthodox cave
monasteries of Inkerman and Eski-Kermen.

The mod treats TDR as the answer to "what if Theodoro had survived
Mehmed II's 1475 conquest and consolidated as a Crimean kingdom of
mixed peoples." The formation event lets you pick which heritage to
foreground: Hellenic-Roman or Crimean Gothic. The two choices
literally rename the country.

The tag is TDR (after Theodoro / Mangup) because BSP is unavailable
in vanilla EU5 and TDR matches the principality's most durable
historical name.

## Forming it

The formable accepts Greek-group orthodox countries.

1. **Be in the Greek culture group.** This includes greek,
   pontic_greek, griko, and gothic cultures.
2. **Be Orthodox.** The `potential` requires `religion:orthodox`.
3. **Own Theodoro.** The seat of Mangup. FEO starts with it; any
   other starter must take it first.
4. **Hold 90 percent of the crimea_area.** A tight fraction: you
   need almost all of the peninsula's locations.
5. **Form TDR.** It is Level 2 plausible.

FEO is the intended path. TRE has the right culture and religion
but starts on the southern Black Sea coast and must project north
across the sea. BYZ runs are essentially a Byzantine restoration
that absorbs Crimea, viable but very long.

## Pick your founding doctrine on formation

The formation event offers two 25-year doctrines. Each renames the
country, swings a societal value, and gates one of the destiny
paths.

- **Regnum Bospori** (heirs of Mithridates and Rome). The Hellenic-
  Roman framing: an heir of Eupator and Mithridates VI that can
  speak with Constantinople and Venice as a peer. Country renames
  to "Regnum Bospori" with adjective "Regnal Bosporan". Pushes
  traditionalist_vs_innovative right (toward innovative). Gates the
  Pontic Reach destiny path (Path A, Heir of Mithridates line).
- **Cimmerian Bosporus** (kingdom of mountain peoples). The Crimean
  Gothic framing: a mountain kingdom of mixed peoples bound by
  Orthodox faith and the iron of Mangup. Country renames to
  "Kingdom of the Cimmerian Bosporus" with adjective "Cimmerian".
  Pushes quality_vs_quantity left (toward quality). Gates the
  Steppe Frontier destiny path (Path B, Ecumenical Throne line).

## What happens on formation

- Mild stability bonus.
- Country rank rises to Kingdom if currently Duchy or below.
- The chosen doctrine renames the country (Regnum Bospori or Kingdom
  of the Cimmerian Bosporus).
- Formation event fires with the two doctrine choices.

## After forming

Tier 1 heritage covers the principality's foundational identity:
the Mangup Citadel (cliff fortress), the Komnenoid (Gabras) dynastic
claim, the Tauric emporion (Cherson, Caffa, Soldaia trade), the
Crimean Gothic tongue, and the cave monasteries. Each comes with a
DHE and a 15-year modifier choice.

The destiny tree splits two ways:

- **Path A: Heir of Mithridates.** Black Sea hegemony line. Stage 1
  takes Sinope and Amasra (the southern Pontic ports). Stage 2 takes
  Trebizond and Varna (Black Sea Mastery), with a Grain Convoy or
  Pontic Customs modifier. Stage 3 capstone takes Constantinople and
  Burgas with a Mithridatic Fleet or Pontic Basileia modifier.
  Capstone building is the **Mithridatic Admiralty**. Unlocked by
  the Regnum Bospori formation choice.
- **Path B: Ecumenical Throne.** Multiethnic steppe-and-Rus' empire
  line. Stage 1 takes Tana and Sarai al-Jadid (the Don and Volga
  gateways). Stage 2 reaches into Kyiv (Ruthenian Outreach), with a
  Dnieper Road or Phanariot Chancery modifier. Stage 3 capstone
  takes Beljamen, Dymer, and Tokat with a Komnenoid Chancery or
  Steppe Basileia modifier. Capstone building is the **Imperial
  Chancery**. Unlocked by the Cimmerian Bosporus formation choice.

## Notes

- The 90 percent crimea_area fraction is tight. Losing a single
  location to a Tatar or Genoese raid can break formation
  eligibility until you take it back.
- Both formation choices rename the country, so the tag in the
  ledger and the displayed name will diverge after formation. The
  TAG remains TDR internally.
- The destiny paths are deliberately divergent: Heir of Mithridates
  is a Mediterranean naval push toward Constantinople; Ecumenical
  Throne is a continental steppe-and-Rus' push toward Kyiv. The
  formation choice gates which one you can take.
